Project Title: Smart Care Sync
Chosen SDG: Good Health and Well-Being (SDG 3)
Vision Statement: Smart Care Sync is a software that aims to ensure patients' health is protected, to better establish connections between patients, hospitals and doctors, and to improve medical efficiency. Our vision is to create a digital outpatient management system to manage appointment booking, and ensure medications, doctor orders are tracked and reminders are sent automatically to the patient, enhancing overall health outcomes in hospitals.
Problem Statement: Through research and observation of past medical experience, we have found that some patients forget their appointment or follow-up visit dates due to busy daily routines; or many patients coming for follow-up visits still need to register at the registration desk along with new patients, causing congestion at the hospital entrance. In addition, the tracking and management of patient medication by hospitals and doctors is very weak, making it impossible to accurately know whether patients are taking their medication on time and in the correct dosage, and patients may also miss doses or take the wrong dosage.Furthermore, the traditional paper-based prescription system is extremely inconvenient. Each time a patient visits, a significant amount of time is spent waiting for nurses to retrieve past medical records and prescriptions from their files. Paper prescriptions are also very inconvenient for patients, as they are kept by the hospital, and patients must copy the doctor's instructions each time; if they forget certain instructions, they cannot access the prescription.
Target Users: 1) Primary Users: Outpatients 2) Secondary Users: Doctors, Hospital Administrative Staff
Product Features: 1) Appointment booking system 2) Follow-up scheduling 3) Medication reminder 4) Digital prescription access
Value Proposition: Smart Care Sync integrates all essential outpatient processes into a unified digital platform, enhancing the patient experience and improving hospital efficiency. Patients no longer need to worry about missing appointments or forgetting medication, as the system provides automatic reminders and follow-up notifications. Doctors can easily track patient treatment adherence, viewing prescriptions or lab results without manually filling out paper forms, allowing them to focus more on patient care. Hospital administrators also benefit from smoother scheduling and less congestion at the registration desk. In short, the system strengthens communication between patients, doctors, and hospitals, ensuring timely treatment, improving medication adherence, and enhancing overall health.
Scope and Boundaries: 1) Included in the system: Online appointment booking and follow-up scheduling for outpatients, automated medication reminders and adherence tracking, digital access to doctor prescriptions and lab orders, dashboards for doctors and administrative staff to monitor patient progress and schedules 2) Excluded from the system: Billing, payment, and insurance claim management, inpatient ward or emergency department management, pharmacy inventory or supply chain systems, advanced diagnostic tools or AI-based medical recommendations
How this software allign with SDG 3: Smart Care Sync directly supports Sustainable Development Goal 3 by promoting reliable access to healthcare and encouraging patients to adhere to their treatment plans. The system helps prevent health complications and supports preventative care by reducing no-shows and providing timely medication reminders. Physicians and hospital staff can efficiently track patient progress, improving the quality and reliability of healthcare. Digital tracking of prescriptions and lab reports ensures patients receive accurate medical guidance, reducing errors and improving safety. Through these improvements, Smart Care Sync brings healthier lives and greater overall well-being to patients in general hospitals.

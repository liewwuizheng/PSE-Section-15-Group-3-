Project Title: Smart Care Sync
Chosen SDG: Good Health and Well-Being (SDG 3)
Vision Statement: Smart Care Sync is a software that aims to ensure patients' health is protected, to better establish connections between patients, hospitals and doctors, and to                       improve medical efficiency. This software can help to manage appointment booking, and ensure medications, doctor orders are tracked and reminders are sent                             automatically to the patient.

# Project Planning: Smart Care Sync

## 1. Project Overview
Smart Care Sync is a digital outpatient management system developed to support the **Sustainable Development Goal 3: Good Health and Well-Being**. This system aims to improve appointment and follow-up management, medication tracking and reminder, and digital prescriptions accessibility for outpatients in general hospitals.

This project will be developed by using **Agile Scrum methodology** over two sprints within four weeks.

## 2. Project Goal
By providing a seamless digital ecosystem, ensuring 100% compliance with follow-up appointments and medication plans, we improve outpatient health outcomes and hospital efficiency, directly supporting Sustainable Development Goal 3 to achieve quality healthcare for all.

## 3. Development Methodology
The development team adopts Agile Scrum to ensure iterative and incremental development. Work is divided into two sprints:
*   **Sprint 1:** Appointment and Follow-Up Management Features
*   **Sprint 2:** Medication Tracking and Reminder, Digital Prescriptions Features

Weekly iterative reviews and retrospectives are held to assess progress and identify areas for improvement.

## 4. Team Roles and Responsibilities

### Product Owner (PO)
*   Leads group formation and role assignments.
*   Selects the SDG goal for the project.
*   Develops the product vision and initial product backlog.
*   Approves final prototype designs and bug fixes.
*   Reviews all technical setups, design work, and documentation.

### Scrum Master (SM)
*   Organizes the Trello Kanban Board.
*   Facilitates sprint planning and retrospective meetings.
*   Updates burn down chart and project tracking tools.
*   Manages the final project evidence submission.
*   Supports the team during research and presentation rehearsals.

### UI/UX Designer
*   Creates wireframes and UI sketches.
*   Participates in all sprint reviews and planning sessions.
*   Collaborates on the visual elements of the presentation slides.

### Prototype Lead
*   Builds early and improved functional prototypes.
*   Fixes identified bugs and mockup issues.
*   Leads the finalization of the technical product.
*   Provides inputs for the test plan and documentation.

### Documentation and Testing Lead
*   Manages the GitHub repository and folder structure.
*   Authors the test plan and test report.
*   Compiles the full project report and documentation.
*   Prepares presentation slides and organizes team's evidence.

## 5. Project Timeline

### Week 1 (Planning)

### Week 2 (Sprint 1)
*   Design appointment and follow-up management features
*   Conduct sprint review and retrospective

### Week 3 (Sprint 2)
*   Design medication tracking and reminder, and digital prescription features
*   Conduct sprint review and retrospective

### Week 4 (Finalization)

*   Prepare report and Presentation

## 6. Used Tools
*   **GitHub:** Version control and documentation evidence

## 7. Risk Management
*   **Potential Risk:** Time constraints, uneven task distribution, and technical issues.
*   **Moderate Solution:** Clear task assignment, regular communication, and sprint reviews for early issues and defections detection.

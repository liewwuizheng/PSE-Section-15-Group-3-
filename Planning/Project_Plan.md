1. Project Overview:
   Smart Care Sync is a digital outpatient management system developed to support the Sustainable Development Goal 3: Good Health and Well-Being. This system aims to improve appointment and follow-up management, medication tracking and reminder, and digital prescriptions accessbility for outpatients in general hospitals.
   This project will be developed by using Agile Scrum methodology over two sprints within four weeks.

2. Project Goal:
   By providing a seamless digital ecosystem, ensuring 100% compliance with follow-up appointments and medication plans, we improve outpatient health outcomes and hospital efficiency, directly supporting Sustainable Development Goal 3 to achieve quality healthcare for all.
  
3. Development Methodology:
   The development team adopts Agile Scrum to ensure iterative and incremental development.
   Work is divided into two sprints:
   - Sprint 1 ~ Appontment and Follow-Up Management Features
   - Sprint 2 ~ Medication Tracking and Reminder, Digital Prescriptions Features
   Weekly iterative reviews and retrospectives are held to assess progress and identify areas for improvement.
  
4. Team Roles and Responsibilities
   - Product Owner (PO): a) Leads group formation and role assignments.
                         b) Selects the SDG goal for the project.
                         c) Develops the product vision and initial product backlog.
                         d) Approves final prototype designs and bug fixes.
                         e) Reviews all technical setups, design work, and documentation.
   - Scrum Master (SM): a) Organizes the Trello Kanban Board.
                        b) Facilitates sprint planning and retrospective meeting.
                        c) Updates burn down chart and project tracking tools.
                        d) Manage the final project evidence submission.
                        e) Support the team for during research and presentation rehearsals.
   - UI/UX Designer: a) Creates wireframes and UI sketches.
                     b) Participate in all sprint reviews and planning sessions.
                     c) Collaborates on the visual elements of the presentatio slides.
   - Prototype Lead: a) Builds early and improved functional prototypes.
                     b) Fixes identified bugs and mockup issues.
                     c) Leads the finalization of the technical product.
                     d) Provides inputs for the test plan and documentation.
   - Documentation and Testing Lead: a) Manages the Github respository and folder structure.
                                     b) Authors the test plan and test report.
                                     c) Compiles the full project report and documentation.
                                     d) Prepare presentation slides and organize teams evidence.

5. Project Timeline:
   - Week 1 (Planning): a) Finalize product vision
                        b) Create product backlog
                        c) Set up Github and Trello 
   - Week 2 (Sprint 1): a) Design appointment and follow-up management features
                        b) Conduct sprint review and retrospective 
   - Week 3 (Sprint 2): a) Design medication tracking and reminder, and digital prescription features
                        b) Conduct sprint review and retrospective
   - Week 4 (Finalization): a) Final prototype completion
                            b) Prepare report and Presentation

6. Used Tools:
   - Github: Version control and documentation evidence
   - Trello: Task management and progress tracking
   - Figma: System prototype development
  
7. Risk Management:
   - Potential Risk: Time constraints, uneven tasks distribution, and technical issues
   - Moderate Solution: Clear task assignment, regular communication, and sprint reviews for early issues and defections detection.

# Final Project Summary: Smart Care Sync

## 1. Overview
**Smart Care Sync** is a  digital health ecosystem designed to bridge the communication gap between hospital administration and outpatients. The project successfully evolved from a foundational appointment system in Sprint 1 to a high-fidelity clinical management and medication adherence platform in Sprint 2.

---

## 2. Objectives
*   **Centralize Administration:** Create a single source of truth for hospital staff to manage appointments, doctor schedules, and daily reports.
*   **Empower Outpatients:** Provide tools for patients to track medication adherence, view digital prescriptions, and monitor lab results.
*   **Enhance Clinical Decision-Making:** Equip doctors with real-time adherence analytics and risk indicators to improve patient outcomes.

---

## 3. Scope
The project was executed over two distinct development sprints:
*   **Sprint 1 (System Foundation):** Account security, core appointment lifecycle (booking/canceling), and basic role-based dashboards.
*   **Sprint 2 (Clinical Integration):** Medication lifecycle (prescriptions/reminders), lab status tracking, and advanced administrative analytics.
*   **Boundaries:** The prototype focuses on the **User Interface (UI) and logical workflow** within Figma; back-end database integration and live telehealth video streaming were excluded from this phase.

---

## 4. Deliverables
*   **Interactive Prototypes:** High-fidelity Figma designs for Outpatient, Doctor, and Administrative Staff roles.
*   **Clinical Feature Set:** 25 verified functional requirements.
*   **Documentation:** Comprehensive Sprint Notes, User Testing Reports, and this Final Summary.

---

## 5. Timeline
*   **Week 1-2 (Sprint 1):** Established core infrastructure. Successfully resolved "Safety Net" issues for appointment cancellations and improved UI readability.
*   **Week 3-4 (Sprint 2):** Transitioned to clinical features. Iterated on "Data Density" feedback to include "Repeat Prescriptions" and "Visual Reporting" toggles.
*   **Final Milestone:** Project successfully reached **Feature Complete** status by the end of Week 4.

---

## 6. Stakeholders
*   **Outpatients:** Primary users of the medication tracking and reminder system.
*   **Doctors:** Clinical leads who utilize the Adherence Dashboard and Digital Prescribing tools.
*   **Hospital Administrative Staff:** Oversight team managing doctor availability and hospital-wide reporting.
*   **Project Team:** A collaborative unit of 5 members utilizing **Github** for version control,  **Trello** for task synchronization and **Figma** for collaborative design.

## What is Smart Care Sync?
Smart Care Sync is a centralized digital ecosystem designed to replace fragmented manual processes in hospital outpatient departments. 

The platform connects **Patients, Doctors, and Admin Staff** in real-time. Whether it's a patient setting a medication reminder, a doctor reviewing adherence trends, or an administrator balancing hospital schedules, the system ensures that data is accessible, accurate, and actionable.

## Our Development Journey (SDLC)
We adopted an **Agile Scrum** methodology, breaking the project into two distinct sprints to move from a "Stable Foundation" to a "Functional Clinical Tool."

### 1. Planning & Requirement Gathering
We started by identifying the "pain points" for each user archetype. We defined our scope to focus on the high-impact areas: scheduling logistics and clinical follow-up.

### 2. System Analysis (The Logic)
Using **Use Case Diagrams**, we mapped out every interaction—from the moment a patient registers to the second a doctor issues a digital prescription. This ensured our logic was sound before we ever drew a single screen.

### 3. System Design (The Blueprint)
Our architecture is built on a robust **UML Class Diagram**. We mapped out the relationships between entities like `Staff`, `Appointment`, `Prescription`, and `LabTest` to ensure the system could scale as data grows.

### 4. Interactive Prototyping
We used **Figma** to bring our ideas to life. Our prototype isn't just a series of images; it’s a clickable journey that covers:
*   Seamless Registration & Role-Based Login
*   Automated Medication Reminders & Adherence Tracking
*   Digital Prescription & Lab Order Management
*   Admin Dashboards with Real-Time Reporting

### 5. Testing & Continuous Improvement
We didn't just build it; we broke it. Through two rounds of **User Testing**, we gathered feedback that led to critical improvements, such as color-coded statuses for admins and "Repeat Prescription" shortcuts for doctors.

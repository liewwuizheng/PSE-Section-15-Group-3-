# Prototype Feedback and Evaluation (Sprint 2)
* **Project:** Smart Care Sync
* **Focus:** Medication Tracking, Digital Prescriptions, and Clinical Reports  

## Evaluation Objective
To validate the clinical data flow among the three prototypes, the focus was on the accuracy of **medication lifecycle**, **laboratory integration**, and **administrative reporting**.

## Stakeholder Feedback

### Outpatient Module
* **Advantages:** Users found the **Mark as "Taken"** check-in system highly satisfying. The **Prescription Summary** provided much-needed clarity on dosage instructions compared to traditional paper notes.
* **Disadvantages:** Users felt the **Track Lab Status** was useful but wanted to see the actual results if the status was "Completed."
* **Improvements:** Added a "Download Result" button for completed lab tests.

### Doctor Module
* **Advantages:** The **Adherence Dashboard** successfully visualized patient compliance, allowing for data-driven consultations. **Digital Prescriptions** significantly reduced the time spent on manual data entry.
* **Disadvantages:** Doctors found the **Add Prescribed Medications** interface lacked a "Duplicate Previous" function, making it tedious for patients on long-term repeat prescriptions. Additionally, the **Adherence View** didn't distinguish between patients who missed one dose versus those who stopped frequently.
* **Improvements:** Added a "Repeat Last Prescription" shortcut for one-click renewals. Refined the adherence analytics to include a "Risk Level" color indicator (e.g., Red for missed doses frequently) to help doctors prioritize critical follow-ups.

### Hospital Administrative Staff Module
*   **Advantages:** The **Search Patient Records** feature by name and phone number was noted for its high speed and accuracy.
*   **Disadvantages:** Additionally, the **Generate Daily Reports** tool provided raw data but lacked a visual summary (e.g., charts) for quick executive review.
*   **Improvements:** Added a **Visual Analytics** toggle to the reporting module that generates instant bar charts for appointment volume and adherence trends.

## Conclusion
With the completion of Sprint 2, **Smart Care Sync** has successfully met all core requirements. The prototype has evolved to a **fully integrated hospital platform** in Sprint 2. By resolving the final feedback regarding data density and administrative efficiency, the system is now considered **Feature Complete** and ready for final project handover and deployment.
